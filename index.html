Our Story

More Than a
Coffee Shop

For over four decades, White Raven has been a sanctuary tucked along Highway 9 in the redwoods of Felton. What started as a neighborhood gathering place has grown into a beloved institution — a spot where locals come for the coffee and stay for the community.

Larry's house-blended chai — slow-simmered from whole spices every morning — is the stuff of legend across Santa Cruz County. Paired with Peerless Coffee pulled with care, and pastries baked fresh daily, every visit feels like coming home.
